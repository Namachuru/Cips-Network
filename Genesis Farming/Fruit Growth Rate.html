Fruit Growth Rates

Different fruits take diffentent times to grow. Understanding the growth rates can help farmers make a decision as to which fruit they would like to grow. In some cases it is better to grow fruit that takes a long time to grow as other farmers are reluctant to grow fruit that takes much longer to mature.
